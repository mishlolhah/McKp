Jeff was one of the competitors in SOS.
His famous catchphrase "My Name Jeff" was not only his nickname,
but also one of the only things he was able to say.
Dr. Mosquito attempted to team up with other survivors,
but Jeff seemed to be the only one who hasn't muted him.
Mosquito waited for Jeff on the bridge, who promised to be on his way to his position.
When Mosquito went into his presumed direction Jeff shouted he's getting shot at.
The next thing Mosquito heard was a male voice claiming he just killed Jeff.[1] Mosquito acted as he would team up with the murderer,
only to stab him to death in the moment he lowered his guard.[2] Mosquito died himself later, but at least he was able to avenge Jeff's death.
